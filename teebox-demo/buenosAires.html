About Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires is the capital and largest city of Argentina. The city is located on the western shore of the estuary of the Río de la Plata, on the southeastern coast of South America. Buenos Aires is known for its European architecture, rich cultural life, and vibrant nightlife.

The city is divided into 48 barrios (neighborhoods), each with its unique character. From the historic streets of San Telmo to the modern waterfront of Puerto Madero, Buenos Aires offers a diverse urban landscape.

Popular Attractions

  • Casa Rosada - The iconic presidential palace
  • Teatro Colón - One of the world's best opera houses
  • La Boca - Colorful neighborhood and home to the Boca Juniors soccer team
  • Recoleta Cemetery - Historic cemetery with elaborate mausoleums
  • Plaza de Mayo - The city's main square and political center
