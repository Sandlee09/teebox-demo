About Denver

Denver, the capital of Colorado, is a vibrant city located at the base of the Rocky Mountains. Known as the Mile High City because its official elevation is exactly one mile above sea level, Denver offers a blend of outdoor adventure and urban sophistication.

With 300 days of sunshine per year, Denver is a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The city boasts over 200 parks and dozens of golf courses. Downtown Denver is home to museums, restaurants, and cultural attractions.

City Highlights

  • Denver Art Museum - Renowned for its collection of American Indian Art
  • Red Rocks Park and Amphitheatre - Natural rock formation that serves as a concert venue
  • Denver Botanic Gardens - 23 acres of plants from around the world
  • Denver Zoo - Home to over 4,000 animals representing 600 species
  • Larimer Square - Historic district with shops, restaurants, and nightlife
